I am a designer studying computer science and visual arts in Victoria, BC.

Flow Internship / May to August 2015

I completed a summer internship with MetaLab, doing design work for Flow. I found my fit by producing designs for Flow’s marketing team, helping out with the workload of Flow's awesome lead designer, Emma. I was happy to contribute during such an exciting time at Flow; I helped the team prepare for a huge product launch that September (Flow Chat).

Media Installation / November 2015

My extended media practices course was assigned to create a generative system, and showcase this work in a gallery environment. I made a web project that scraps youtube video ID’s from twiiter’s API. The user was to input random statements into the system, and try to make sense of the results.

Logo Design / October 2015

A logo mock up I designed for my brother. He was starting up an aerial media company and was looking for a simple logo and watermark for his footage.

Sound Art Installation / November 2015

My extended media practices course had a project assignment to create a sound art installation. I decided to create an interactive piece using web sockets, so that each viewer could contribute to the piece using their own device.
