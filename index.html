I am a designer studying computer science and visual arts in Victoria, BC.

Flow Internship / May to August 2015

I completed a summer internship with MetaLab, doing design work for Flow. I found my fit by producing designs for Flow’s marketing team, helping out with the workload of Flow's amazing lead designer, Emma. I was happy to contribute during such an exciting time at Flow; I helped the team prepare for a huge product launch that September (Flow Chat).

Media Installation / November 2015

My extended media practices course featured an assignment to create a generative system, and showcase this work in a gallery environment. I made a web project that scraps youtube video ID’s from twiiter’s API. The user was to input random statements into the system, and try to make sense of the results.

Logo Design / October 2015

A mock up logo design for an aerial media company that hasn't gotten off of the ground yet. The company was going to be based in Sylvan Lake, AB. Their first client was a wake-surf champion looking for a new promotional video.

Sound Art Installation / November 2015

My extended media practices course had a project assignment to create a sound art installation. I decided to create an interactive piece using web sockets, so that each viewer could contribute to the piece using their own devices.
